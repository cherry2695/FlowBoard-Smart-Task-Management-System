About Flowboard

Flowboard is a modern and intuitive task management platform designed to help individuals and teams plan, organize and track their daily work with ease. Whether you're managing personal goals, academic assignments, or professional projects, Flowboard provides a clean and user-friendly workspace to create tasks, assign priorities, monitor progress, and meet deadlines.
